Recipe: How to Make Biryani Instantly

Discover how to create delicious Biryani in just 25 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 500g basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2-3 tablespoons ghee or vegetable oil
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 tablespoon biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 cups chicken broth
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves for garnish
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk (optional)
  • Fried onions for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, boil the soaked rice in salted water until 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. In another pan, heat ghee and sauté the sliced onions until golden brown. Set aside a portion for garnish.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add the chicken pieces, turmeric powder, and biryani masala. Cook until the chicken is half-cooked.
  5. Stir in chopped tomatoes, yogurt, and salt. Cook for 5-10 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  6. In a large ovenproof pot, layer half of the cooked rice, then add the chicken mixture. Add the remaining rice on top.
  7. Drizzle saffron milk over the rice, and sprinkle garam masala, fresh coriander, and mint leaves.
  8. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Garnish with fried onions, and serve hot with raita or a side salad.
