Recipe: How to Make Masala Dosa Instantly

Discover how to create delicious Masala Dosa in just 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice, soaked overnight
  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram), soaked overnight
  • 1 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water for grinding
  • Ghee or vegetable oil for cooking
  • Potato masala (optional, for masala dosa)
  • Chutney and sambar for serving

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds.
  2. Grind the rice and urad dal separately with some water to a smooth, thick batter.
  3. Mix both batters together in a large bowl, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for 8-10 hours.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or a cast-iron griddle and grease it lightly with ghee or oil.
  5. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the pan and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin dosa.
  6. Cook until the edges begin to lift and the bottom is golden brown. Flip and cook the other side for a minute.
  7. If making masala dosa, add a spoonful of potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.
  8. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
