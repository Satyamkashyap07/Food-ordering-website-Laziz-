How do I place an order?

To place an order, navigate to the 'Foods' section, select your desired items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

We accept major credit/debit cards and popular online payment platforms. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you can track your order through your account dashboard. You will also receive email updates about your order status.
