Recipe: How to Make Chocolate Pastery Instantly

Discover how to create delicious pastery in just 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 200g plain flour
  • 100g unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1-2 tablespoons cold water
  • 100g dark chocolate, melted
  • 50ml heavy cream
  • 50g sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, butter, and icing sugar. Rub the butter into the flour with your fingers until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
  2. Add the egg yolk and a tablespoon of cold water. Mix until a dough forms, adding more water if needed.
  3. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  5. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface and cut into desired pastry shapes. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the pastries are golden brown. Allow them to cool on a wire rack.
  7. While the pastries are baking, prepare the chocolate filling by combining melted dark chocolate, heavy cream, sugar, vanilla extract, and a pinch of salt. Mix until smooth.
  8. Once the pastries have cooled, fill them with the chocolate mixture. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
