Recipe: How to Make Crispy noodles Instantly

Discover how to create delicious Crispy noodles in just 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 200g dried noodles (egg noodles or rice noodles)
  • 3-4 cups vegetable oil (for deep frying)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 large carrot, julienned
  • 100g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2-3 spring onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoons water (slurry)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook the dried noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
  2. In a deep pot, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Ensure the oil is hot enough for deep frying.
  3. Carefully drop small batches of the cooked noodles into the hot oil. Fry until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  4. In a separate pan, heat a small amount of oil and sauté the bell pepper, carrot, and mushrooms until tender.
  5. Add the soy sauce and oyster sauce to the vegetables and stir well.
  6. Add the cornflour slurry to thicken the sauce, then add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. To serve, place the crispy noodles on a plate and pour the sautéed vegetables over them.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy!
