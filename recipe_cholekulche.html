Recipe: How to Make Chole kulche Instantly

Discover how to create delicious Chole kulche in just 15 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (chole), soaked overnight
  • 2-3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Kulche (Indian flatbread)

Instructions

  1. Drain and rinse the soaked chickpeas. Pressure cook with water and salt for 15-20 minutes, or until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat the oil and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for another minute.
  4. Add tomatoes, green chili, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  5. Add the cooked chickpeas to the pan and stir to combine.
  6. Cook for 10-15 minutes, adding a little water if necessary to prevent sticking.
  7. Add garam masala and stir well.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  9. Serve hot with kulche.
